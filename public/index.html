About me

Get to know me!

Hi, I'm Rupesh, often found as @roopxx online. My journey with technology started in early school days, where I enjoyed dismantling gadgets and creatively repurposing their parts. Even back then, I found joy in building websites for fun. Although I initially pursued Pharmacy in college due to a fascination with drugs, my exposure during high school also made me appreciate the service-oriented nature of pharmacy, fostering meaningful interactions with patients and customers.

Simultaneously, I developed a parallel interest in Computer Science, embarking on a self-taught journey into full-stack development in 2021 with The Odin Project . Along the way, I discovered a particular passion for JavaScript and React.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I maintain my sanity with a diverse range of hobbies, including reading books, gardening, and learning languages (currently diving into German). I occasionally find myself spontaneously writing short poems and essays inspired by my prevailing emotional state.

When not immersed in coding, you'll likely find me engrossed in a Kindle book or navigating the vast realm of the internet. I firmly believe in never stopping personal growth, fueled by a passion for technology and an unwavering desire to push the limits of what is possible. Excited about the trajectory of my career, I remain open to new opportunities. 🙂
